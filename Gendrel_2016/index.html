    Gendrel, Atlas & Hobert 2016

    A cellular and regulatory map of the GABAergic nervous system of C. elegans

    Gendrel M, Atlas EG, Hobert O
    eLife 5:e17686
    Published: 14 October 2016

    Abstract

    • Reports a comprehensive neurotransmitter map of neurons in the C.elegans connectome revealing twice as many GABA-positive neuron classes as previously reported.
    • This data was later used and incorporated into Bentley et al. 2016 multilayer connectome and Randi et al. 2023 functional connectome.
